About Kenya

Kenya is one of Africa’s most iconic tourist destinations. From the Great Rift Valley and Mount Kenya to the endless savannahs and Maasai Mara, it’s a paradise for wildlife lovers and cultural explorers.

Top Destinations

  • Maasai Mara – Great Wildebeest Migration
  • Diani Beach – White sandy beaches
  • Lake Nakuru – Flamingo haven
  • Mount Kenya – Climbing adventures

Culture & Activities

Discover diverse tribes like the Maasai, Samburu, and Swahili. Enjoy music, traditional dances, local foods, and craft markets. Kenya also offers safaris, hiking, diving, and hot air balloon rides.
